ABOUT US

Westmeadows Medical Centre was first established in 1972 initially with a solo practitioner and in late 2007, the clinic was taken over by three dedicated medical practitioners. Over the past 46 years, our clinic has provided exceptional medical services to thousands of patients in the Northern suburbs of Melbourne. We have been a well-known family oriented medical practice in the community and our relationship with our patients is built on the foundation of trust, professional approach and delivering high quality of health care. As an accredited medical centre with QPA, we take the responsibility of providing quality patient outcomes. The professional conduct of our health team and staff is of highest standards and always guided by the best practice principles.
